Getting Started with Building a Website

By James McEwen | October 20, 2020 | Building Websites

When it comes to building a website and having a solid web presence on the internet there are a number of factors to consider and in this post I am going to list a few of the items for you.

Picking the Right URL or Domain Name

Step one is picking the right URL or Domain name. You don't want to pick something that is too long or too hard to spell because this is what will happen. When it's too long people may not remember it so keep it short and easy. Picking a domain that is hard to spell is another bad thing to do, remember not everyone out is a great speller so if they misspell your domain they won't find you.

What Are You Building the Website For?

Remember the reason why you are building this website and also remember that 98% of your traffic is going to come from handheld devices like phones or tablets, so you may have a great looking website on your desktop or laptop but make sure that it is a MOBILE RESPONSIVE design. Think to yourself what are you building the website for?, for yourself or for your end users.

UX is Key but Content is Key as Well

We have discussed the importance of having a great UX design and the importance of the flow of your website. Another key to having a successful online presence is CONTENT. Content is not King but it's very relevant when wanting to climb the ranks of SEO. Having content that is related to your business is extremely important.
